Services

A quick overview of our services.

Web Design

Your website is a representation of your business, having a website that does not represent your business is one of the main reasons for a business to fail. All websites in our opinion should be the main priority we starting or growing your business. At FalDigital we design, build and host all types of websites, all sites we create are user-friendly and fully responsive. The websites that we create are perfect for whatever device the user is using i.e. laptops, tablets, mobile and desktops.

Social Media

The world social media has become a very important part for any business starting. Social Media is one of the most powerful marketing tools for any business as it is now a part of everyone’s daily life. Here at FalDigital we fully understand the benefits of Facebook, Twitter, Instagram and Google Ads, we offer a social media management for businesses who want to promote their business. Also for start-up businesses we offer social media set up which includes a tutorial session so that you are able to understand all platforms.

SEO

We understand that everyone wants their website to rank well with the major search engines such as Google. Unfortunately there is not a golden bullet ....but if you ensure your site carries out good coding protocol then you will be able to gain a good listing without having to try and fool the search engines with link farms. There are many elements to consider when building the site and we offer our clients the "best practice" procedures to ensuring their sites are optimised just as the search engines request. All our SEO services offer insight reporting so you can see progression and real time results.

Branding

Creating a recognisable brand for your business plays a huge part in starting or growing your business, we create modern branding that can represent your business. We take your imagination or idea and then bring it to life, making the brand to exactly how you want it too look.

Advertising

Advertising is a way of marketing your business in order to increase sales or make your audience aware of your products or services. Until a customer deals with you directly and actually buys your products or services, your advertising may help to form their first impressions of your business. Advertisement could be through Social Media adverts, YouTube adverts, or even the basic leaflets.
